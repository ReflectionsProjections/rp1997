About the Conference
The University of Illinois Student Chapter of the ACM is proud to announce the Third Annual Midwestern ACM Chapter Conference. The previous two conferences were a resounding success, and we are eager to invite everyone back for more. The official theme for this year's conference is again "Reflections | Projections".

Like last year, ACM@UIUC hopes to attract both speakers and attendees that are interested in the insight of past, present and future visionaries in computer technology. The conference is open to everyone willing to register. No ACM or industry affiliation required, simply an interest in computers and technology.
